Mission & Vision

The Budda Bears is a unique collection of 8,888 digital collectible NFT's living on the Ethereum blockchain. As a member of Budda Bears, you will not only own a cute and unique digital asset, but will also gain access to a wide range of exclusive benefits and events. These include in-person and virtual experiences, discounts on cannabis, VIP experiences and the opportunity to be a part of a positive and dynamic community.

Our mission is to cultivate a better culture for cryptocurrency, cannabis, and music, and we invite you to join us on this journey by holding a Budda Bear. Your support will help us promote the use of innovative technologies and create a brighter future for all.

In addition to our focus on cultivating a better culture, we also have a strong commitment to growing our brand on a global scale. We are constantly exploring new partnerships and collaborations to expand our reach and bring the joy of Budda Bears to people all over the world. By owning a Budda Bear, you will not only support our mission, but will also be a part of our journey to become a world-renowned brand. Together, we can make the Budda-verse a vibrant and exciting place for all
